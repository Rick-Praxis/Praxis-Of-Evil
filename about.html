Hello and welcome to Praxis Of Evil I'm Rick and I enjoy writing about any variety of things including fiction, satire, culture, and politics, with an air of insufferable douchebag ironic detachment.

My work explores themes surrounding being 40 years old and wasting a years and years saying racist bullshit on the internet while my life collapsed around me, but at least I made brown people mad.

When I'm not writing, I'm slaving away as a goy trapped in a wage cage yearning to break free, Lord Yakub did not intend me to live this way.
